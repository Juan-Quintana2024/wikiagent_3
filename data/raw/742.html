Nanjing Tech University (南京工业大学) is a provincial public technological university in Nanjing, Jiangsu, China. It is affiliated with the Province of Jiangsu.[2] The school was established in 2001 by the merger of the former Nanjing Chemical Engineering University (南京化工大学) and the former Nanjing Architectural Engineering College (南京建筑工程学院).[3]
