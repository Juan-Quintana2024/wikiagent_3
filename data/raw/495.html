The Nanjing–Luoyang Expressway (Chinese: 南京—洛阳高速公路), designated as G36 and commonly referred to as the Ningluo Expressway (Chinese: 宁洛高速公路) is an expressway that connects the cities of Nanjing, Jiangsu, China, and Luoyang, Henan. It is 1,354.7 km (841.8 mi) in length.

The expressway was fully completed on 30 September 2006. It passes through the following cities:[1]

  • Nanjing, Jiangsu
  • Bengbu, Anhui
  • Fuyang, Anhui
  • Zhoukou, Henan
  • Pingdingshan, Henan
  • Luoyang, Henan
