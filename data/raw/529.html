Baijiahu station (Chinese: 百家湖站; pinyin: Bǎijiāhú Zhàn) named after Baijia Lake nearby to the northwest, is a station of Line 1 of the Nanjing Metro. It began operations on 28 May 2010,[1][2] as part of the southern extension of line 1 from Andemen to CPU.
