Lu Keran (Chinese: 陆柯燃; Korean: 루커란; born Lu Jie [陆婕]; November 7, 1995), better known by her stage name K, is a Chinese singer and dancer. She is the leader of girl group Fanxy Red.[1][2] She is also a former member of Chinese girl group The9,[3] after finishing ninth in iQIYI's survival reality program Youth with You 2.

Early life

[edit]

Keran was born on November 7, 1995, in Nanjing, China. She played competitive fencing for six years, and even went to a special athletic academy to be a competitive fencer.[4]
